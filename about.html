Our Proven Process

We don’t come in with cookie-cutter ideas. Real estate sales are an art your team has honed over years and decades. Our job is to take that art, digitise it, and scale it. Here’s how we do it.

Our Proven Process
We sit with your sales team and understand their pitch in detail —the language they use, the hooks that work, the objections they handle.
Every project has its own rhythm, and we make sure our campaigns echo that.
We don’t invent USPs in a vacuum. We pick the strongest hooks from your own team—what has been working on the ground with walk-ins and phone calls—and translate that into ad copy, creatives, and landing page flows.
Once we have the pitch and USPs, we create:
  • Ad scripts and landing page copy
  • Video shoots (optional), which may include walkthroughs, influencer pitches, or storytelling edits
  • Short edits for Meta and Google campaigns
Everything is shared for your approval before we launch.
We launch across Google, Meta, and other major platforms with your approved scripts and creatives. All leads flow directly into your CRM or Google Sheets—real time, no delays.
We monitor lead feedback daily. Sales team thinks a lead is fake or invalid, or not reachable? We adjust campaigns instantly—messaging, targeting, or filters. This keeps CPL dropping and quality rising. And that’s why we ask our clients to provide us with feedback on leads within 24 hours.
If a lead turns out to be broker, junk, or invalid, we replace it proactively—often before you even ask. This keeps your pipeline clean and your sales team focused only on genuine buyers.

24-Hour Lead Feedback Policy

We deliver every lead to you in real-time —via CRM or Google Sheets. To keep conversion rates high, we require your sales team to share feedback within 24 hours .

Leads that sit uncontacted beyond 24 hours go cold , reducing the chances of site visits or bookings.

Your timely feedback helps our campaign managers optimise targeting and improve both lead quality and volume.

Only leads marked as invalid or fake within 24 hours are eligible for replacement . Beyond this window, they are automatically considered qualified.

A faster feedback loop that protects your ROI and ensures campaigns stay sharp, relevant, and high-performing.
